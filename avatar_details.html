Research Overview

The Avatar Model represents a paradigm shift in AI agent architecture, moving from externally orchestrated systems to integrated, self-contained autonomous entities that own their identity, state, and capabilities.

This research addresses fundamental limitations in current AI agent designs, proposing a modular "Parts" architecture that enables true agent autonomy while maintaining the power of external LLMs for reasoning.
